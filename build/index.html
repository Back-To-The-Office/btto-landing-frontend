About Service

Back To the Office is an application which increases accessibility of employees.

All contacts of each team members are in one place. Back To the Office is useful to ask advice from a colleague from another department or urgently contact the accounting department. Employer does not wait for an answer from a manager or colleagues from the chat.

Issues are solved faster, employees communicate directly and share experiences.

  • Teams

    Employees are divided into teams in Back To the Office application. The position, method of communication and working hours are dislayed in each team member.

    It increases employees communicative availability and allows them to find each other faster.

    Back To the Office lets beginners to accustom to the team better.

  • Structure of the Company

  • Time Zones

    The graph shows time which employee has in different time zones. It will help all large companies departments to socialize around the world.
